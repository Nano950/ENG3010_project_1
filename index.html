The Discouse Community of Software Developers




The purpose of this research guide is to introduce the concept of software developers as a discourse community according Swalesâ€™ criteria for discourse communities. This guide accomplishes this task by looking at how developers use various communication practices to share information, as well as how the various textual and discursive elements of these practices are used to accomplish this task. This guide also discusses one of the major conversations happening within the software development discourse community through describing some of the academic literature on the topic, and how the works of various authors contribute to the development of this conversation.
